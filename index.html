Apple Production (2010-2020)

China has produced around 40 million tonnes of apples troughout 2010-2020. This makes them the first largest apple producer troughout 2010-2020. Fuji apple makes around 60% of the apple production in China (Wang, Wolf and Zhang, 2016).

Banana Production (2010-2020)

Reaching up to 30 million tonnes production, India has ranked number 1 as the largest banana producer in the world.

Orange Production (2010-2020)

Oranges are mostly produced in Brazil, with around 16 million produce troughout 2010-2020.
